Ye'abasha Gomen

Description

Ye'abesha Gomen is a traditional vegetarian Ethiopian dish made by sauteing collard greens with garlic, ginger, and an aromatic infused oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 green bell pepper minced
  • ½ bunch green onions sliced
  • 1 onion minced
  • 1 bag collard greens
  • ½ jalapeno pepper seeds removed
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste

Steps

  1. In a large pan on medium heat, add infused oil, then 1 tbsp each of garlic & ginger paste. Saute for a minute before adding the bell pepper and onions, then green onions and jalapeno pepper.
  2. Saute for about 5 minutes and then stir in collard greens, in increments, until all the collard greens have reduced by about half.
  3. Serve warm or room temperature.
